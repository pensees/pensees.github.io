These thoughts are intended to be as universal as possible. Different people have different opinions and it is perfectly fine.

If you disagree with a thought, please don't be afraid to say so and explain why. We'll accept your suggestion if you can make a valid and rational case for it. Please avoid subjective thoughts, for example arguing that green is the best color doesn't make sense.

The ultimate goal of this project is to come up with a collection of universal thoughts and life lessons aimed to help humanity lead a happier and healthier life, both as individuals and together as a species.
